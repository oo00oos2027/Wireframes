Four core rules
The entire fulfillment plan derives from these four decisions.
RULE 01
Expedited-first when selected
If expedited shipping is toggled on at the time of adding to cart, the system allocates as many units as the expedited cap allows — before touching standard inventory.
RULE 02
Standard-first otherwise
Without expedited, standard allocation absorbs units up to its cap. Expedited stays at zero regardless of available capacity.
RULE 03
Backorder is always the remainder
Backorder = qty − standard − expedited. It is read-only and recalculates instantly on every quantity or allocation change.
RULE 04
Plan is hidden until requested
Default cart view shows only the summary pills. The editable breakdown is on-demand — expand per line item only when an override is needed.
